pivLib.PIV1

pivLib.PIV1#

pivLib.PIV1(I0, I1, winsize, overlap, dt, smooth=True)#

Wrapper of openpiv.pyprocess.extended_search_area_piv(), with an option to smooth the resulting velocity field.

Deprecated since version 1.0: This function does not have the frequently required validation and outlier replacing routine, and is replaced permanently by pivLib.PIV().
